Reinventing roadside. To help achieve this goal, we’ve thought about features and services customers want and applied them to an experiential experience we call… Spork.

No, “Spork” is not the product’s true name. It describes the experience we want for customers. One Brand... One URL... Spork is multifunctional, but with simplified engagement.

User research and testing are helping us better understand the needs and desires of customers. The intention of this prototype is to spark interest in reinvention and encourage collaborative innovation. See for yourself. Contact UX&D, feedback is encouraged.
